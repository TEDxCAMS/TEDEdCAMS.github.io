What is TEDxYouth
@CAMS?

TEDxYouth@CAMS is the California Academy of Mathematics and Science's first annual TEDxYouth event. During a 12-hour period in April 2017, speakers from both CAMS and the world will share their ideas. Join us for a day of education and suprise about the opportunities that are created when we think about starting up!
